Taman Penelitian Coral Sense

Program 2 · Research

Sebulan yang memperkaya pendidikanmu, CV-mu, dan cara berpikirmu sebagai ilmuwan masa depan.

Jika kamu sedang belajar biologi kelautan, konservasi, atau ilmu lingkungan — ini adalah jalur yang lebih dalam. Kamu menyelam lebih sering, mengamati terumbu sebagai sistem, dan mulai memperhatikan hal-hal yang dulu terlewat.

Minimum 4 minggu·$1,680 / bulan·Mahasiswa biologi kelautan & awal karier
Yang membuat ini berbeda: Eksperimen langsung — efek substrat, variabel kedalaman, kompetisi spesies. Kamu belajar membaca terumbu karang sebagai sistem, dan bagaimana alat berbantuan AI mendukung penelitian lapangan nyata.

Who it's for

  • Students of marine biology, marine conservation, environmental science, ecology
  • Early-career participants stepping into reef restoration and marine research
  • Divers ready to grow from recreational diving into disciplined field observation

What you'll actually learn

  • How a real restoration project works under operational conditions
  • Reef care, structure maintenance, and restoration decision logic
  • Photo protocols, anomaly spotting, basic monitoring methods
  • Coral group identification and reef dynamics observation
  • How AI-assisted tools support field material analysis
  • The difference between recreational diving and science-oriented observation

Requirements

  • Academic or early-career background in a related field
  • Open Water certification (can be completed locally)
  • Personal laptop · Ability to ride a scooter

What's included

  • 16 dives in 4 weeks — restoration & science-oriented
  • Full dive equipment
  • Training sessions: coral restoration, marine conservation, research thinking, AI workflows
  • Both gardens: Amed Research Garden + Padang Bai
  • 30 nights accommodation — shared room included; private room & villa available at extra cost · Inter-site transfer
  • 2 lunches per week on dive days
  • Airport pickup and drop-off · Mask, fins, snorkeling gear, SUP
  • Coral Sense team t-shirt · Certificate of Completion
